FAQ
Questions
Answered.
Will you lowball me?
▼
We make fair offers based on comps, acreage, access, and utilities. We're building a business on referrals — that only works if sellers feel good about the deal.
Do I need a real estate agent?
▼
No. We buy directly from owners. No MLS, no commission. You keep every dollar of our offer.
What if there are liens or back taxes?
▼
Tell us upfront — we can often still make it work. Liens and back taxes can typically be resolved at closing and deducted from the purchase price. We handle the details.
How fast can you actually close?
▼
Standard is 30 days or less. We've closed in as little as 7 days. If you need more time, we work around your timeline.
Do you buy in every state?
▼
Yes. All 50 states. Rural, suburban, raw acreage, residential lots, agricultural land — if it's land, we're interested.
Is there any obligation when I submit?
▼
Zero. Submitting gets you an offer. Accept, counter, or walk away — no pressure, no harassment.
What do I need to submit?
▼
Name, phone, property address or APN/Parcel ID, acreage, and a rough asking price if you have one. That's it. We do the rest of the research.
